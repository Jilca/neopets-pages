Character Profile

Name: Ozymandias Flores
Age:42
Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
Gender: male
Pronouns: He/him
Occupation: Mechanic/town drunk
Residence: New Friendship
Theme song: The Antlers - Putting the Dog to Sleep
Personality:
Oz used to be a very kind and proud person who would constantly try to do good by others. Oz, as he is now, is nothing more than a shell of his former self. Passive aggressive, uncaring, and otherwise macabre to be around, Oz is rarely seen around NF sober. When he is, he's working, which is enough for some people, but not for everyone.

Quirks:
- Oz is from the Junklands, like Flashbang.
- He knows how to fly a plane, specifically a two person Cessna.
- He does not like robots.
- He used to be a soldier for the Naturalist Core before it was disbanded.
- He's a survivalist, and knows the most about how to survive in Nowhere compared to everyone else in the Gang.
- He is really into botany, and could probably tell you what each plant in Nowhere can do to you.
- Very fond of poems and literature, and is pretty wellspoken when sober.

Setbacks:
- He's a huge jerk.
- He's an alcoholic.
- He hates Arcanists.
- He refuses to change.
- He hates relying on people, and as a result, tends to isolate himself.







About Nowhere

NowhereAU is an alternate timeline of Neopia, where the Faeries leave Neopia behind for unknown reasons, taking all the magic with them. Neopia spends decades trying to replace the magic that once held their society and lives together by using what came from the planet itself -- coal, petrolium, gas -- anything they could get their hands on, until a mysterious new powersource was found.

Neopylite.

This strange crystal seemed to give off the same magic that the Faeries did. Strife surrounding this crystal and it's usage ensues, resulting in an all out war between those who are pro-Neopylite (Arcanists) and those who are anti-Neopylite (Naturalists). This war ends in the worst way possible, the total destruction of both sides.

180 years later, society has begun rebuilding itself, slightly more lawless and grimy than before. This is when the AU takes place, in the burnt out apocalyptic lands of Neopia, now affectionately renamed Nowhere.







History

Oz was born into a family of scribes and archivests, displaced into the Junklands after the Rust began to take control over the Arcanist controlled regions of Nowhere. He grew up always learning and eager to understand complex concepts. Although he was very bright, he ultimately became caught up in the Naturalist Core, a group that will not let go of a potential member once they've sunk their claws into them. Oz was essentially drafted, more or less. He spent the better part of his teen and young adult years learning about the planes the Naturalist Core prided itself over. He was reluctant at first, but ultimately came to enjoy it, and loved the sense of community it offered.

After the Naturalist Core was disbanded, he took a plane and left the Junklands, feeling as if it had nothing left to offer him. He landed in an embankment outside of Lucien and lived out of his plane for quite a while until Finch, Metz, and Esther came across him. He decided to travel with them, mostly because he intended to rob them blind in the dead of night, but also because he craved the sense of belonging he lost after the Disbandment.
