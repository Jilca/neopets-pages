Background Info:

Odun is an ancient deity of immense strength and discipline. His passion had always been for knowledge and the pursuit of power; and with it, freedom. Freedom from his father's tyranny, and the chains of immortality. Centuries were spent keeping his head down, whilst in privacy he silently honed is strength and powers. Eventually, the day came in which his plans to assassinate his father were to come to fruition. However, in attempting to do so, he accidentally took the life of his beloved brother, Adiel. Enraged at such a betrayal, Odun's father banished his son, and Odun was forced to reside in the nightmares of mortals for all eternity. At first, Odun is appalled and anguished by his fate, but later learns to accept the hand he'd been dealt and make the most out of it, else he fears he'd lose his mind. Though twisted, Odun's only exposure to the outside world is through watching and observing nightmares.




Odun is an introvert in every sense of the word. Rather than bring attention to himself, Odun has always preferred to isolate himself from others and observe in silence. Although he did not enjoy the company of most, Odun was fiercly loyal and protective of his younger brother, Adiel. Odun is not what one would call an emotional being. Unlike his father, he is thoroughly even tempered and logical-minded.
