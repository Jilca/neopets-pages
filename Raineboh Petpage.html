Try to be a rainbow in someone's cloud

Hello and welcome to my petpage! My name is Raineboh! I'm the little sister of Bakerey but I'm mostly seen with my best friend, Muiteto! We both live up in an island floating in the sky! I have many friends who I consider family and they influence how I am now! I'm filled with happiness and joy and hope to spread that to everyone I know!









Profile

Raineboh grew up living with Bakerey since she's her little sister! As she grew older she met wonderful new friends (Muiteto, Galaxz, Pretza, etc) and she followed them everywhere! She began to love stars, the weather, and all sorts of plants! (inffluenced by her friends!) She then decided to live on the sky island with Muiteto so she could be closer to her passions! There she learned to create rainbows with the help of Muiteto's magical lake! Now Raineboh uses her power to create rainbows whenever someone is feeling down. Her hope is to bring happiness to those who are having a hard time.
