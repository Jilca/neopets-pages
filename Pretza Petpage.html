Hello and welcome to my petpage! My name is Pretza! I'm an alien kitty who loves growing different types of flowers! I even have my own little garden on planet Earth! In my garden you can find all sorts of flowers/plants from all over the universe! It's definitely an out-of-this-world garden!







Profile

Pretza is actually an alien kitty born on a planet faaaaaar from Earth. On her home planet, she grew a love for gardening. She is fascinated with growing all sorts of flowers/plants. To expand her flower/plant collection, she decided to travel to planet earth where she can discover new things to grow! She now lives with Ekieno and has made her own little garden on earth!









Likes


➔ She loves gardening! Her goal is to grow many different types of plants/flowers!
➔ Her favorite flower is the star flower! She LOVES stars!
➔ She looks up to Ekieno and loves spending time with her.

Dislikes


➔ She doesn't like when things try to ruin her garden. (animals eating her fruits, her trouble maker family members walking over her garden, etc)
➔ She doesn't like windy/stormy days. She prefers sunny days to tend to her garden!
