What does she want most in life? (Not objective)
To end the Noxian war so she can sit in her garden in Ionia, tending to her flowers and drinking tea.

Why?
Planting flowers with her grandmother was her favourite activity as a child, but when the Noxians invaded, they destroyed her home and slaughtered her entire family as she hid in the shelter of the garden. She was too scared to move for days afterwards, staring at the blood-laden leaves and uprooted flowers in shock. She tried to save the garden, which was all that remained of her home, but nothing survived.
