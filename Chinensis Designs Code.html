Frequently Asked Questions

  • What program are you using to edit your graphics?
    I currently use Photoshop CS5 to edit them.
  • How often do you take requests?
    It all depends on my schedule in real life. Everything at school is quite hectic right now. I do make sure, however, that I open it as much as possible or as soon as I see time to do requests or graphics.
  • Can my site be an affiliate or be a sister site of Chinensis Designs?
    As for the moment, I'm accepting high-quality graphics or any kind of request sites as my affiliates. Because of that, not all affiliate applications may be accepted. As long as affiliation requests are open, you are free to submit applications.
  • I really like your layout. Do you accept layout requests?
    Thank you! Unfortunately, I don't code my own layouts. Although I am a bit familiar with HTML/CSS codes, I can't really be trusted when it comes to making layouts or so. I can, however, help or collaborate to build a customized layout. Just neomail me, if you are interested.
  • Can you teach me how to...?
    Unfortunately, I can't teach you how to do specific effects on graphics (especially animations on buttons) since I'm still learning how to do them myself. There are great sites out there with tutorials to help you out, so do take the time to look at them. I may, on the other hand, give you tips on how you can improve your graphics (if you want me to, that is).

Origin of the Name

Chinensis Designs was derived from the flower, Rosa Chinensis which is more known as the China Rose in English. It is a member of the genus Rosa native to Southwest China in Guizhou, Hubei, and Sichuan Provinces (Wikipedia). Being a big fan of the anime/series, 'Maria Watches Over Us' (マリア様がみてる), Yoosshi decided to use the name for her site as she, herself, likes roses and finds the name mysterious.

The past

Formerly named as 'Haibane Designs,' Chinensis Designs was created by Yoosshi (atrociology) in 2005. Along with her co-owner and real-life friend Munette, they designed multiple graphics and layouts for Xanga-users. After few months, the site itself was placed into indefinite hiatus because both owners became busy with school. Inevitably, it was closed down. In Summer 2008, Yoosshi alone decided to create an extension of Chinensis in Crunchyroll as she finally found the time to make graphics (as well as she really liked the community). She did, however, have 6 other moderators to help her out. Again, because of the lack of time to maintain the group, it was closed soon after.

Where graphics bloom into art

As of the 15th of October 2011, Chinensis Designs was officially launched here in Neopets and requests were opened for Neopians. It was placed in indefinite hiatus in Spring 2012 to let her focus on her studies. It was, long after, re-opened on the 12th of October 2013.
