Hi! I'm Evelyn!

I'm a 19-year-old university/college student studying video game design! I was born on Feb, 23rd, making me a pisces. It's really fitting because I flop around like a fish a lot IRL! I also do other weird things like dab, which started ironically but now it's impulsive. Yes, my friends hate me for it.

In my free time I listen to instrumental music and try to get myself to write, make art, or read. The stuff I write goes on my petpages, the things I make go on my IG, and the books I read go straight to my soul. Exploring, learning, and creating are what I live for.

Pound Chat is the only board I visit and I usually lurk to see if there are any nice names around or if there's any piping hot tea for me to absorb like a drama-less sponge.

Otherwise, I'll be chatting with my beautiful friends (in alphabetical order) Annie, Ashe, Claire, Charlie, Elle, Emily, Mely, Morgan, Nana, Stephanie, and Tiffany off-site. I love my friends with all my heart and they're the ones who keep me inspired, hardworking, and happy. I love you, if you're reading this. I really friggin love you.

    FAMILY

    These are all my current permanent pets! That means they are not UFT/A. I love them all too much to give them away, and they all have individual characters/stories. Click on each little symbol at the end of their description to view their petpages!


    Occula the ???

    I've had Occula for so long I've nearly forgotten how I got ahold of her LOL. I traded a plushie cybunny I had for an FFQ custom. I saw someone post the name 'Occula' on an untaken names board and I knew I had to have it, so I asked the trader to create her and paint her transparent. Transparent Aishas are highly popular for customizing and Occula was no exception. I didn't really have a character in mind for her, she was just there because I enjoyed her name and appearance. After having her for years, I finally figured it was time to give the girl a purpose, so I came up with the outfit you see on her now. I'm also going to use her character development as an excuse to watch lots of horror movies hehe. ♪


    Sang the ???

    Sang was my first trade in a long, long time. I traded my uc faerie pteri for him, which was my dream pet originally, but I could not connect with her name for the life of me. I advertised her fleetingly in search for something to replace her character. Eventually, I came across Sang, who reminded me of a character I had for Imbue (a draik I used to own) and was also the French word for "blood". I was also engrossed in Cryaotic's playthrough of Vampyr at the time and I knew I wanted the name with every fibre of my being.
    I offered on Sang and was considered for a few days. I didn't expect to hear back about him, given the PC's track record, but needless to say, the owner eventually messaged me saying they'd like to trade. I was stoked out of my mind and I can't wait to see where his character goes! ✧


    Orele the Omen Ogre

    Orele's character is one of those rare ones that originated from a customization I made on DTI. I saved it because I loved the shenkuu-themed attire on the stealthy cybunny, but I never thought I'd ever be able to obtain all the wearables, especially the background but I got extremely lucky and nabbed it from someone discounting his closet! Thus, I managed to get all the items before adopting Orele from the lovely Elizabeth who offered her to me on Feburary 1st, 2016!

    Orele is one of the pets I've had for the longest (second to Nimli). The feeling of being starstruck when I first adopted her and my love for her unique, dynamic character always stuck around and Orele will be evolving with me for years to come! ☾


    Oriandra the Runaway Royal

    Oriandra is a princess who never obeyed her royal duties. She believed in freedom and rebellion above all, but also secrecy and silent betrayal. She constantly snuck out of her palace to visit her only friends: the forest creatures.

    Ori was eventually caught disguised as a peasant woman, and banished from her crown for good. Without a moment's comfort for celebration of her permanent freedom, she meets a prince who requires a royal bride. He awoke something inside her for the first time: love. Thus, Ori runs back, in order to be accepted back into the place she swore her life against. ♚

    Wicke the Outlaw Fae

    I had been obsessed with Wicke's name ever since I found her UFA sometime in 2016 (I believe). I had less than a day to apply at that point so I only got so far as to make the header before I gave up (ya girl needed sleep ok?). I searched Wicke up again after I returned from my first year of university while discussing pet names with my friend Tani. As it turns out, she was on an active account! I messaged the owner if they would be willing to trade her, and they were! I rounded up some offers but didn't really have anything worthy of Wicke. I planned on trying to find a nice 4L to offer but life took me by the reins again and I was swept up in other matters for a while. After almost giving up hope, my friend Lise decided to gift me her uc tyrannian mynci to offer on Wicke. My offer was accepted and I hand-painted her from a pirate draik into a wraith usul not long after, intending for her to take after another wraith usul I used to own. ☁

    Blessy the ???

    Blessy was a gift from my dear friend Tani! I reconnected with her after my hiatus and she decided to give the little bab to me after I told her about some names I really liked but couldn't obtain (Wicke being one of them in fact!). She messaged me offering me Blessy and I graciously accepted in a heartbeat. I wanted to give her the outfit I had planned years back for a cloud gnorbu but never completed due to the items being too valuable, but was a little on the fence as the items didn't get any easier to obtain. However, with Tani's excitement for it, I was settled. It took a long time to trade for everything but eventually the customization was complete. I was overjoyed with completing a dream outfit of mine and it renewed my love for this site. Even without anything to do, I simply enjoy logging on to admire Blessy sometimes and lurk the boards. This gnorbu has honestly been nothing short of a blessing. ❤

    Muskiteer the Grandmaster Duelist

    Wow. I'm still speechless every time I look at Muskit because no part of me believes I would ever be able to obtain such a pet. He was a gift from the incredible Morgan, a friend I reconnected with after about 2 years. She was frozen out of nowhere one day, cutting off our one method of contact. I found her again off-site and we rekindled our friendship. She had been busy with IRL things during our time apart but had recently come back to Neopets because she couldn't let go of the site. One of her accounts that didn't get frozen had Muskiteer on it, and for some incredible, surreal reason I cannot fathom, she decided to entrust me with him. I never knew I could love the little guy so much but I do, and immediately started revamping my application for a uc grey ixi to befit Muskit. However, I later realized I wanted his character to be more upbeat and fantastical than a grey pet's, so he's going to be turned into a heartthrob of a duelist! ♛

    Frihtz the ???

    Frihtz was an amazing gift from the beautiful, lovely Nana! I knew her briefly over two years ago when we met through our mutual friend Claire. We hadn't spoken to each other since, but met again through Morgan. We three became close immediately, chatting every day for weeks. Nana was browsing through uc pets and remembered how much she loved the baby kougras and wanted the three of us to have matching ones. She decided to trade her uc faerie aisha for one, gifted one to Morgan, and of course gave Frihtz to me. I never had a baby pet (aside from Nimli, who hosts my portfolio) because I was absolutely atrocious at making characters for them, but Frihtz captured my heart and inspired me to push through my comfort zone. I love the direction his story is going to take, and it combines elements of a novel I've been wanting to write since grade 9, so it will hold incredible sentimental value. ☆

    Desires the ???

    Desires is yet another gift from Morgan! She was in the process of rebuilding when my friend, Mely, offered her Desires to adopt. Morgan accepted and planned on using her to trade up, but decided to give her to me because she knew of my inhuman love for real words. I also had a character for her already but didn't have a befitting name for (though the story was a little inappropriate for Neopets anyway huehue). Regardless, Desires has made me extremely excited to revisit an old OC, one that I loved very much, and finish her story! I intended to use it to explore and write about feminism, something that sneaks its way into many things I write but never had a proper outlet for until now! ✘
